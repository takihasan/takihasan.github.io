Taki Hasan Rafi

Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, and Healthcare Researcher.

I am working with Prof. Young Woong as an Assistant Researcher on Deep-Hash based Similar Image Retrieval and Storage System at Operating System Laboratory, Hallym University. But now, we are developing a multi-head attention model for ECG classification. Prior to this, I received Bachelor's Degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology this June 2021. I am broadly interested in developing deep learning methods for medical applications. My undergrad thesis work was focused on Environmental Sound Recognition with Spiking Neural Network. I previously developed an Electroencephalogram (EEG) Classifier using Extreme Gradient Boosting algorithm and PCA. I also developed an Ensemble Deep Learning model for Chest X-Ray Image classification. Besides research, I worked at Summit Communication Limited as an Apprentice Engineer. Prior joining to Hallym University, I recently left working as a STEM Educator at SciTech Innovations Limited, where I contributed developing a student-friendly STEM curriculum first time in Bangladesh. This working environment allows me to work with a different group of communities. Which surely be a thrilling and highlighted experience.
