Bio

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Hanyang University (HYU), where I am working at the Data Intelligence Lab with Prof. Dong-Kyu Chae. Before joining HYU, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh in 2021. My Ph.D. is supported by Brain Korea 21 (NRF) and Samsung Electronics! I'm a research collaborator at Oracle, USA.

My research primarily focuses on Trustworthy AI, to build reliable, robust, and safe intelligent systems.

    Reliability and Robustness: Test-Time Adaptation, Domain Generalization, and Graph Learning. [CIKM 2025, DASFAA 2025a, DASFAA 2025b, ACCV 2024]
    Safety: LLM and Multimodal Models, Bias Assessment and Mitigation, and Low-Resource Languages. [ACL 2025a, ACL 2025b, NAACL 2025]
