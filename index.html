Taki Hasan Rafi

Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, and Healthcare Researcher.

I will be graduating from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology this June. I am broadly interested in developing machine learning methods for high-dimensional data using conventional statistical approaches. My current work is focused on Spiking Neural Network on Environmental Sound Data. I previously developed an Electroencephalogram (EEG) Classifier using Extreme Gradient Boosting algorithm and PCA. I also developed an Ensemble Deep Learning model for Chest X-Ray Image classification. Besides research, I worked at Summit Communication Limited as an Apprentice Engineer. I recently left working as a STEM Educator at SciTech Innovations Limited, where I contributed developing a student-friendly STEM curriculum first time in Bangladesh. This working environment allows me to work with a different group of communities. Which surely be a thrilling and highlighted experience.

I am actively looking for a PhD position in ML/CV/AI or other closely related fields. If you are interested to hire, please email (takihasanrafi@gmail.com) me to know more.
